About the Founder

Building the Future of Healthcare

Eeswar Makineni is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of MET-Ai Solutions, a healthcare AI venture founded in Amaravathi, Andhra Pradesh. His work began with a single question: why does one of the world's most critical industries still rely on manual, disconnected, and error-prone processes?

That question led him to build MET-Ai, combining the precision of medical electronic technology with the intelligence of artificial intelligence to automate, predict, and transform every aspect of hospital care for patients across India and beyond.

Every hospital deserves the most intelligent, automated, and accurate system that technology can build. That is what MET-Ai exists to deliver.
  • Healthcare AI and intelligent hospital automation
  • Medical electronic technology and device integration
  • Machine learning and predictive health analytics
  • Hospital workflow design and digital transformation
  • Product vision and technology leadership
