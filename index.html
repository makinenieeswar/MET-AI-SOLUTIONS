About MET-Ai Solutions

Built to Revolutionize
Modern Medicine

A visionary Indian startup redefining what hospitals can achieve with the power of AI — proudly rooted in Bharat.

"Our mission is total automation of healthcare — from the moment a patient walks in to the moment they recover, AI handles it all."

MET-Ai Solutions (Medi Electronic Technology – Artificial Intelligence) is a startup on a bold mission: to eliminate inefficiency, human error, and delayed care from every hospital in India and beyond. We are building the intelligent nervous system that future hospitals will run on.

Our platform unifies patient intake, AI-powered diagnostics, treatment planning, medication management, remote monitoring, and hospital operations — all seamlessly automated into one ecosystem.
